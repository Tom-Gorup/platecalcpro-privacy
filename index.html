Plate Calculator Pro

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

tomsappco ("we," "us," or "our") built Plate Calculator Pro (Bundle ID: com.tomsappco.platecalcpro) as a commercial iOS application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

Plate Calculator Pro does not require an account, does not use first-party analytics or tracking, and does not transmit any data to our servers. The app functions fully offline.

All user data — including your settings and saved weights — is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We display banner advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • General location (derived from IP address)
  • Device and app interaction data for ad personalization

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to the data Google collects through AdMob.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, or by using Google's ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

In-App Purchase (Remove Ads)

Plate Calculator Pro offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Once you purchase the ad-removal option, AdMob is disabled and no third-party advertising data collection occurs.

Children's Privacy

Plate Calculator Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect any personal data ourselves, no special measures are required. However, the third-party advertising provided by Google AdMob may collect device information as described above. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child is being served personalized ads, you can disable ad tracking in your device settings or purchase the ad-removal option.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing for us to retain. Locally stored app data on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy
